Style Guide

This is the style guide for GamerHub

Visceral

Designing for first impressions – covering information architecture, layout, typography and colour.

Behavioural

Designing for functionality – covering interactive components, micro-interactions and feedback.

Reflective

Designing for meaning and identity – covering how users reflect on and ascribe value to the site over time.
